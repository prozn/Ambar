Warm support for young learners

Playful, thoughtful teaching support for children.

I believe that every child deserves to feel valued, understood, and confident in their ability to learn and grow. With over 11 years of experience teaching in UK primary schools, I have had the privilege of supporting children from a wide range of backgrounds, abilities, and experiences.

Throughout my career, I have developed a particular passion for inclusive education, emotional wellbeing, and helping children build the confidence they need to flourish both in and beyond the classroom. I have extensive experience supporting children with Special Educational Needs (SEN), English as an Additional Language (EAL), and those who benefit from additional emotional or pastoral support.

My training in child counselling and emotional support has deepened my understanding of children's emotional development and the important connection between wellbeing and learning. I know that children make the greatest progress when they feel safe, listened to, and encouraged to be themselves.

Whether I am working with a child to develop their academic skills, strengthen their confidence, or navigate emotional challenges, my aim is always the same: to provide a warm, nurturing, and supportive environment where they feel empowered to discover their strengths and reach their full potential.
